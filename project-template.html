Customer Churn Analysis

Predicting customer churn using machine learning models to identify key risk factors and improve retention strategies.

Python Pandas Scikit-learn Looker Studio

Problem Solved

The company was experiencing a high rate of customer attrition but lacked visibility into the underlying causes. The goal was to identify at-risk customers and understand the drivers of churn.

Methodology & Tools

  • Data cleaning and preprocessing using Pandas.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to uncover patterns.
  • Feature engineering and model selection using Scikit-learn.
  • Visualization of results in Looker Studio.

Key Findings & Insights

  • Customers on monthly contracts are 2.5x more likely to churn.
  • Lack of tech support is a significant predictor of dissatisfaction.
  • Fiber optic internet users have a higher churn rate due to service reliability issues.
